Trellathon

Trellathon is the largest, one of its kind, hackathon for GGSIPU students. While most of the hackathons focus on code, we at Trellathon consider the uniqueness and potential of the idea as well. Trellathon provides a platform for all the innovators out there to come out and test their creation on the market parameters. We aim to separate “a good idea” from a “market product” and fill the gap in that journey.

How is ACM Trellathon different from others?

We believe that ideas are just ideas if they are not feasible. ACM Trellathon analyses not just your coding skills but also the overall potential and growth of your idea.

What shall I expect from ACM Trellathon?

Great insights, exciting prizes and lots of fun.

Are we supposed to use any particular language?

Languages are just a medium to express. Use any language of your choice to implement your idea into reality.

What is the maximum team size?

A team in ACM Trellathon comprises 2 to 5 participants.

Is it a software only hackathon?

Yes, it is a software-only hackathon.

Is there any entry fee?

No, all you need is your creative ideas and coding skills at disposal.

What will be the selection criteria?

Your product will be judged on its feasibility, uniqueness and potential of the idea.

Are inter-college teams allowed?

Yes, the only criteria is that all participants should be college students of institues affiliated to GGSIPU.
